Privacy Policy – Barricade

Last updated: March 2, 2026

Barricade takes your privacy very seriously. This policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and what rights you have as a user.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Barricade does not collect, store, or share personal data such as your name, email address, location, or browsing history.

2. Accessibility

To provide selected app and website blocking features, Barricade requests access to your device’s accessibility service.

  • This permission is only used to detect which apps and websites are being used.
  • No information is sent to external servers. Everything is processed locally on your device.

3. Camera

To provide QR code and barcode scanning features, Barricade requests access to your device’s camera.

  • This permission is only used to scan QR codes and barcodes when unlocking an app or website.
  • No information is sent to external servers. Everything is processed locally on your device.

4. User Rights

  • Right to Information: You may know what the app does with information, even if it is minimal.
  • Right to Transparency: You may access this privacy policy at any time.
  • Right to Withdraw Permissions: You may revoke accessibility permissions from your device settings at any time after scanning the codes you configured.
  • Right to Contact the Developer: For privacy-related questions or concerns, you may email the address listed below.

5. Policy Changes

We reserve the right to update this policy. Any changes will be posted in this section and notified within the app when relevant.
